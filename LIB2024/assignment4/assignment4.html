Converting from Fahrenheit to Celsius

To use this converter:

  1. Enter the temperature in Fahrenheit in the text box.
  2. Click the CONVERT button to see the temperature in Celsius.
  3. To enter a new temperature, click the RESET button.

Celsius:

Do it yourself: (x°F − 32) × 5/9 = y°C
